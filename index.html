Welcome to TapTranslate

TapTranslate is an AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) web application designedto help individuals communicate easily.

It is designed for individuals with speech and language difficulties, such as those with autism, cerebral palsy, or other conditions that affect communication.


With TapTranslate, you can:

  • Tap on images to hear the corresponding words or phrases.
  • Use the word bank to find and learn new words.
  • Communicate effectively with others.
