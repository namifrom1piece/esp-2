About This Project

TapTranslate is an AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) web application designed to help individuals with speech and language difficulties communicate effectively.

Project Goal

The goal of TapTranslate is to provide a simple, accessible tool for individuals with communication challenges, such as those with:

  • Autism spectrum disorders
  • Communication difficulties
  • Self-injurious behaviours
  • Other conditions affecting speech and language

Key Features

  • Image-based communication system
  • Audio playback for words and phrases
  • Organized word banks for easy navigation
  • Queue-based audio system for sentence building
  • History tracking of used words and phrases
  • Responsive design for use on various devices
